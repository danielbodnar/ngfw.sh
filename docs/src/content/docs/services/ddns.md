title: Dynamic DNS
description: Keep your domain pointing to your dynamic IP
---

Dynamic DNS automatically updates your domain when your IP address changes.

## Supported Providers

- Cloudflare
- DuckDNS
- No-IP
- Dynu
- Custom

## Configuration

Enter your provider credentials and hostname. NGFW.sh will automatically update the record when your WAN IP changes.

See the [API Reference](/api/overview) for programmatic configuration.
